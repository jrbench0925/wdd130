Idaho

Idaho is a state in the northwestern United States. Idaho is the 43rd state. It became a state in 1890. The capital city of Boise, located in the western part of the state, is it's largest city. It is bordered by Washington and Oregon to the west, Canada to the north, Montana to the north and east, wyoming on the east, and Utah and Nevada to the south. Idaho may be famous for potatoes but the best part is the scenery and wildlife.
